My Favourite Dish

Chicken Biryani

My favourite Dish is chicken biryani. It is famous in each and every corner of teh world. In each and every Country there are different types of recipes. It is not only my favourite dish but about 70% people love to eat. The cost per plate is ₹ 80/- It is a mixed rice of all the diffrent items. It is the most originating among Hindus ,Muslims and South people of INDIANS. It can also be done with Meat ,Chicken ,Eggs , Beefs and many more.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken
  2. Rice
  3. Vegetables
  4. Ghee
  5. Salt and all Masala
  6. Yoghurt
  7. Yellow and Green colour

Recipe

  • Prepare the chicken curry
  • Boil the rice
  • Bring all the stuff together
  • Make the bottom layer of rice
  • Then again layer of chicken curry
  • Put some Dried Bay leaf
  • And other Masala
  • Repeat the whole cycle for two times
